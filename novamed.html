novamed pharmaceutical
Power bi Sales & customer Analysis

Prepared by: Gabriel Okeke | Data Analyst

Project Overview

This project analyzes NovaMed’s 2023 sales performance and customer behavior using Power BI to provide clear visibility into business operations and support data-driven decision-making. The analysis focuses on identifying key revenue drivers, evaluating product performance, and understanding customer purchasing patterns across different demographics, regions, and buyer types. By transforming raw transactional data into structured insights, the project highlights areas of strength, potential risks, and opportunities for growth within the business.

Tools Used

  • Power BI – for interactive dashboard development and data visualization
  • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – for calculated metrics and performance insights
  • Power Query – for data cleaning, transformation, and modeling

Sales Dashboard Overview

Sales Dashboard Insights

  • Total revenue reached approximately $71.31M with profit of $58.45M, reflecting a strong profit margin of about 82%.
  • Revenue is highly concentrated in a few top-performing drugs, indicating dependency risk on limited products.
  • Monthly sales fluctuate significantly, showing unstable demand and lack of consistent revenue flow.
  • Certain regions and product lines outperform others, suggesting opportunities for targeted expansion.

Customer Dashboard Overview

Customer Dashboard Insights

  • A small group of high-value customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue, with some generating over $600K individually.
  • Adult customers represent the highest revenue-generating segment, indicating strong purchasing power.
  • Male customers contribute slightly more revenue overall compared to female customers.
  • Bulk buyers (sellers) dominate revenue contribution, significantly outperforming regular users.

Business Recommendations

  • Diversify product offerings to reduce dependency on a few high-performing drugs and minimize risk.
  • Implement demand forecasting techniques to stabilize monthly revenue fluctuations and improve planning.
  • Strengthen retention strategies for high-value customers through loyalty programs and personalized engagement.
  • Optimize pricing and product positioning to maximize profitability across different drug categories.
  • Expand focus on high-performing customer segments such as adult buyers and bulk purchasers.
