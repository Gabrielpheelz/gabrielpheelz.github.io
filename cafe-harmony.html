Cafe Harmony
Excel Sales & Operational Analysis

Prepared by: Gabriel Okeke | Data Analyst

An end-to-end data analysis project carried out using Microsoft Excel, focused on evaluating Café Harmony’s sales, operations, and customer experience. Using advanced Excel functions, pivot tables, and data cleaning techniques, multiple datasets were transformed to uncover key performance trends across sales, inventory, employee productivity, and customer feedback.

The analysis identified operational inefficiencies and revenue opportunities, leading to strategic recommendations aimed at improving sales performance, optimizing inventory management, enhancing staff productivity, and increasing customer satisfaction.

Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel
  • Pivot Tables
  • Data Cleaning Techniques

Dashboard Overview

Key Insights

  • Sales declined consistently from $4,683 in 2024 to $3,459 in 2026, with a sharper drop in the most recent year, indicating weakening revenue momentum.
  • Muffin, Cappuccino, and Iced Tea are the top-performing products, each generating over $2,200, while Sandwich and Croissant lag behind by approximately $500–$600.
  • A major performance gap exists among employees, with top performers exceeding targets significantly while the bottom five fall well below expectations.
  • Customers aged 29–39 and 51–61 contribute the highest revenue, while the 62–72 age group shows the lowest engagement.
  • Key ingredients such as Milk, Muffin Mix, and Croissant Dough are frequently understocked, directly affecting the availability of top-selling products.
  • Customer satisfaction is moderate (average rating ~3.0/5), with a noticeable share of low ratings, indicating inconsistent service quality.

Recommendations

  • Address declining sales by targeting high-value customer segments (ages 29–61) with promotions and loyalty programs.
  • Optimize product strategy by focusing on high-performing items (Muffin, Cappuccino, Iced Tea) while improving or repositioning low-performing menu items.
  • Implement structured staff training and performance incentives to reduce the gap between top and low-performing employees.
  • Introduce inventory forecasting and automated restocking to prevent shortages of key ingredients and avoid lost sales.
  • Standardize customer service processes across locations to improve ratings and ensure a consistent customer experience.
